Our phone apps.

Whether you’re collecting Petro‑Points, starting a car wash or charging your electric vehicle, we are with you the whole way. Download our Petro‑Canada app or our Petro‑Canada EV app today.

Petro‑Canada app.

Our favourite features include the ability to:

  • Find a Petro‑Pass cardlock using your location or the city or town name
  • See Petro‑Pass truck stop hours, contact information and a location map
  • Get directions and distance to the location
  • Filter results for fuel preferences and amenities See everything in French as well as English

You can also sign up for notifications so you can hear about updates and special offers.
